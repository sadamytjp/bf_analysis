Ben & Frank Test by Juan Pablo Sada¶

The goal of this analysis will be to present 2018 Q1 sales. To achive our goal we'll need to:

  • Clean the data to get the information we're going to use in our analysis.
  • Determine a way to calculate the week days and weekend days with more sales per store.
  • Determine the peak hours of shopping.

Data Analysis¶

  • Most popular day of the week
  • Average Ticket
  • Peak Hour
